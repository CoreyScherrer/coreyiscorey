Ground rules

Identity documents never live on this site. Scans and photos stay in the family Google Drive folder. Pages here link to Drive — they never hold the files themselves.

Scans are for ordering, not for filing. Croatian authorities require originals or officially certified copies. Every scan in Drive exists so the right physical document can be ordered from the right office.

Where sources disagree, the disagreement is recorded. Open questions are listed with the person who owns them rather than answered by guesswork — a wrong fact here propagates into every document downstream.
