Applicants

Everyone in the family bloc, the ancestral chain each person's claim runs through, and what each still needs. Corrections are proposed here and reviewed by the record keepers.

How corrections work. Anyone can propose a change to their own record — a spelling, a middle name, a date. Proposals go to Paula Radich, Mark Grant, and Mark Sullivan, who apply them to the family record. Nothing changes automatically, so a mistaken entry can never overwrite a verified fact.

Names and lineage only — no dates or document contents are published on this page. Identity documents live in the family Drive folder and are never stored on this site.
